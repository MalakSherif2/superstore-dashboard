# 📊 Insights & Recommendations

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 🌆 City Performance

* New York generates the highest profit among all cities
* Profit is concentrated in major cities, while smaller cities underperform

---

### 📦 Product Performance

* High-profit sub-categories: Copiers, Phones, Accessories
* Loss-making sub-category: Tables
* Bookcases generate very low profit

---

### 🌍 Regional Analysis

* West region leads in both sales and profit
* Central region has the lowest profit margin despite reasonable sales
* East region shows strong and stable performance

---

### 🚚 Shipping Insights

* Standard Class dominates with more than 50% of total sales
* Same-day and First Class shipping are underutilized

---

## 💡 Recommendations

### 📉 Improve Low-Performing Products

* Re-evaluate pricing strategy for Tables
* Consider discontinuing or redesigning loss-making products

---

### 🌎 Optimize Regional Performance

* Investigate high costs or heavy discounts in the Central region
* Apply successful strategies from West region to other regions

---

### 💰 Increase Profitability

* Reduce excessive discounting
* Focus on promoting high-margin products

---

### 🚀 Enhance Shipping Strategy

* Encourage faster shipping options through promotions
* Improve logistics efficiency for better customer experience

---

## 📌 Conclusion

The business is profitable, but there are clear opportunities to improve profit margins by optimizing product strategy, reducing losses, and enhancing regional performance.
